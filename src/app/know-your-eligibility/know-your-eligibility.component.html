Instructions For Checking Scholarship Eligibility

Welcome to the Scholarship Eligibility Checker! Please follow the instructions below to determine which scholarship schemes you are eligible for before starting the application process.

  • Fill in all the required fields accurately.
  • Double-check your entries to ensure there are no errors.
  • Once all the fields are filled, click on the 'Check Eligibility' button to see the list of scholarships you can apply for.
  • This tool is designed to save your time by showing only the scholarships you are eligible for based on the information provided.
  • If you find yourself ineligible for any scholarships, consider revisiting your details to ensure they are correct or click on ‘Why I'm not eligible for this scheme’ to know the reason for your ineligibility.

View eligible scholarship schemes

  • Once you fill in all your details, you will be able to see a list of all the scholarship schemes you are eligible for. Each scheme will be displayed along with its closing date and guidelines to help you understand the application process.
  • Review the closing dates carefully to ensure you submit your applications on time.
  • Read the guidelines thoroughly to understand the application process and required documents.
  • If no schemes are displayed, recheck your details for accuracy or review the reasons for ineligibility provided.
